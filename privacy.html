Privacy Policy

Effective April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dusk Collective LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Spence app ("Spence" or "the app"). We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and name, provided via Sign in with Apple

Pantry Data

  • Ingredients, quantities, and expiration dates you enter
  • This data is stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage

Household Profile

  • Dietary preferences, allergies, and household size
  • Stored locally on your device

Recipe & Cooking History

  • Recipes generated for you and your cooking history
  • Stored locally on your device

Meal Plans & Grocery Lists

  • Any meal plans or grocery lists you create
  • Stored locally on your device

Usage Analytics

  • Anonymous, aggregated data such as app opens and feature usage
  • This data cannot be used to identify you personally

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized recipes using AI based on your pantry, preferences, and dietary needs
  • To provide meal planning and grocery list features
  • To improve the app experience through anonymous usage analytics

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Spence uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — Processes pantry and preference data to generate personalized recipes. Data sent to Anthropic is used only for the API call and is not stored by them beyond processing.
  • Supabase — Provides authentication services and serverless functions for the app backend.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple for account authentication and StoreKit for subscription management.
  • Pexels — Provides food photography for recipe display. No user data is sent to Pexels.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your pantry data, household profile, recipe history, meal plans, and grocery lists are stored locally on your device. They do not live on our servers.

Account authentication is handled through Supabase using encrypted connections. We use industry-standard security measures to protect the data that does pass through our systems.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app
  • Export your data — download your pantry, recipes, and profile data
  • Opt out of analytics — disable anonymous usage tracking in app settings

6. Children's Privacy

Spence is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, reach out to us at daniel@duskcollective.co.
