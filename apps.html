Spence

Dinner's Figured Out.

Spence is your AI-powered kitchen assistant. It tracks what's in your pantry, generates personalized recipes based on what you already have, helps you plan meals for the week, and builds smart grocery lists that fill in the gaps. No more staring into the fridge wondering what to make. No more food going to waste. Spence handles the thinking so you can just cook.

  • Pantry tracking with smart expiration alerts
  • AI-generated recipes from your ingredients
  • Weekly meal planning
  • Intelligent grocery list generation
  • Food waste prevention at its core
